About Us

Welcome to True Collabs, your trusted partner in influencer marketing. Founded by Haseeb Ur Rehman, we are dedicated to connecting brands with the right influencers to create impactful campaigns.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between brands and influencers, fostering authentic collaborations that resonate with audiences and drive engagement.

Our Vision

We envision a world where influencer marketing is transparent, effective, and mutually beneficial for both brands and influencers.

Our Values

At True Collabs, we value authenticity, creativity, and results. We strive to create campaigns that are not only successful but also meaningful and genuine.

About the Founder: Haseeb Ur Rehman

Haseeb Ur Rehman is the visionary behind True Collabs. With a passion for marketing and a deep understanding of the influencer industry, Haseeb has built a platform that seamlessly connects brands and influencers. His dedication to authenticity and creative excellence drives the success of every campaign at True Collabs.
